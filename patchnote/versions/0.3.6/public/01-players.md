# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.6`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

_(Draft — not published.)_

- [CHANGE] **Header announcements:** The bar at the top that can show login streaks and rotating server messages now **fades out** after you have read **every** current message line once (including when it alternates between the streak ticker and each line). It stays hidden for **about ten minutes** if nothing new was added, then can show again on its own. If operators post **new or reordered** lines before that, the bar comes back right away so you do not miss updates.

- [CHANGE] **Main menu:** The big action is now labeled **Enter game**, with a short line underneath that you sign in with your **Nimiq wallet**. Telegram and X sit under a small **Community** heading so they read as social links, not the way into the game.
- [FIX] **NIM reward bar:** If you tap a claimable block from far away, the “move beside the block” strip **fades out after a few seconds** instead of staying forever.
- [CHANGE] **Billboard menu:** The floating **Edit / Move / Delete** controls use the same compact pill look as the **Enter** button when you stand on portals or doors.
- [FIX] **Buttons:** Clicks should line up with the visible button—**no more accidental presses** in empty space around pills (including **Enter** on portals and **Reconnect**).
