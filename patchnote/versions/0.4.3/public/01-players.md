# Public patch notes — players (`0.4.3`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [NEW] **Reorganized right-click menu.** The wheel around your avatar now opens with clear, labelled choices — **Emoji**, **Home**, **Games**, and **1v1**. **Home** has your **Rooms** and your **Private Room**; **Games → Soccer** leads to **Free Play** or **1v1** (play someone in **this room**, or **invite** a friend); **1v1** is a quick shortcut straight to those match options.
- [NEW] **Private play spaces.** From **Home → Private Room** (or **Games → Soccer → 1v1 → Invite**) you now open a private, invite-only room and share its link (or QR). Friends can join straight from the link — wallet optional — pick a nickname, and land right inside with you. Up to eight people can be in one play space at a time.
- [NEW] **Room code button that stays put.** Inside a play space, a **🔗 Room code** button sits in the top bar for everyone in the room. Tap it anytime to bring back the room code, link, and QR — no more chasing a popup that vanished. The share card now opens once when you arrive and can be closed so it never blocks play.
- [NEW] **Run your own 1v1s in the space.** Anyone in a play space can raise a Challenge and play a 1v1; when a match ends both players drop back into the play space, and everyone else can watch from the Spectate Portal that appears there.
- [CHANGE] The play space stays open as long as someone is in it (even while players are away in a match) and quietly closes once everyone has left. The link works for any occupant, so guests can invite more friends too.
- [FIX] Accepting an invite as a guest now drops you **into the play space** instead of bouncing you to the starting chamber.
- Guests are kept to their play space and the matches it starts, and do not earn NIM rewards.
