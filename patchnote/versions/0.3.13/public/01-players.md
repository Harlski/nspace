# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.13`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [CHANGE] **Mine from the world menu** — the **Mine** line shows a short red note that this path takes longer; the hold bar matches that longer time on the server.
- [FIX] **Mine when beside a block** — you can start a claim again with a **normal click** on the block when you are already next to it (or walk closer first), not only from the right-click menu.
- [CHANGE] **Tapped-out mine blocks** — clicking a gold block that is on cooldown shows **“There's no NIM left here :(”** by your avatar instead of doing nothing.
- [CHANGE] **Less clutter** — the old browser tooltip on mineable blocks is gone; sparkles and the menu still show what is special.
- [FIX] **Rapid messages** — quick repeats of the short floating hints by your avatar (for example walk errors) always show the latest line instead of disappearing.
