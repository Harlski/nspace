# Public patch notes — brief (`0.5.3`)

**Audience:** widest reach — social posts, in-app one-liner, “what changed at a glance.”  
**Depth:** minimal; no jargon; link to longer tiers if published.

---

Teleporters are easier to build and use: a **Set** pill on the tile picks where they go, and **Enter** warps you through. This update also adds new achievements, lets you recolor teleporter pillars, and adds a green check on open 1v1 challenges so you can accept with one click.
