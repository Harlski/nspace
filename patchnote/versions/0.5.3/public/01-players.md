# Public patch notes — players (`0.5.3`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

### Teleporters

- [NEW] **Set** on a teleporter tile (build mode) opens a destination picker: choose a room and tap where visitors should land. Cross-room links show a loading screen when you walk through.
- [NEW] **Enter** is the walk-mode pill on doors and configured teleporters. Same-room warps say **Enter**; cross-room warps say **Enter {Room name}**.
- [NEW] On desktop, **Space** opens **Set** when a teleporter is selected in build mode, or triggers **Enter** when you are standing on a door/teleporter offer.
- [NEW] Teleporter pillars use a clean default white tint; room owners can recolor them with the build hue ring (live preview on the pillar and in the inspector).
- [CHANGE] Linked teleporters in the same room: moving one end no longer drags the other — only the tile you move shifts; the pair stays connected.
- [FIX] Teleporters can only be placed on the ground floor or one block up, and only one per floor column — clearer rules and preview ghost while placing.
- [CHANGE] **Guest entry** (where new visitors spawn) moved to **Build → Room → Room settings**, below room background color. The button is now **Set spawner**.

### World Cup (when enabled)

- [NEW] Tap the green check beside someone's **1v1?** bubble to accept their challenge instantly — no need to open the player menu.

### Achievements

- [NEW] **Teleporter Engineer** — configure a teleporter so it is ready to use.
- [NEW] **Room Furnisher** — place 150 blocks in your room using at least 10 different colors.
- [NEW] **Beat the Creator** — win a 1v1 match against the creator wallet.
- [NEW] **They Heard You** — open a feedback ticket that has an admin reply.
- [NEW] **Point Hunter I** / **Point Hunter II** — reach 500 / 1000 total achievement points (new **Meta** category in the achievements panel).
