# Public patch notes — players (`0.4.4`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [NEW] **Co-build in Play Spaces** — when you’re in a private Play Space (Home → Private Room or Games → Soccer → 1v1 → Invite), you and everyone else there can place blocks and recolor the floor for the session. Nothing carries over after the room closes; teleporters and gates still aren’t available there.
- [FIX] **Join codes stay case-sensitive** — 8-character Play Space codes keep mixed letters as typed when joining from the Rooms browser.
- [NEW] **Chat moderation** — inappropriate language is censored in chat; messages that are entirely profanity are blocked and you see a short system notice.
- [CHANGE] **Expired or closed Play Space links** — guests who hit a full, closed, or expired invite see a clearer wallet sign-in path (Nimiq Pay links) instead of a dead-end “Return home” screen; the same card style powers the **Get a wallet** toolbar prompt.
- [FIX] **Share Panel copy** — room code and join link copy buttons work more reliably on mobile and show “Copy failed” with the field selected when the clipboard isn’t available.
