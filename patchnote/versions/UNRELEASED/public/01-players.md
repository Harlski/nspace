# Public patch notes — players (`UNRELEASED`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

**[NEW] Player Menu** — Tap your identicon in the bottom-right corner for Wardrobe, **Shop**, Achievements, Rooms, **Return to Hub**, and Logout (guests see Profile, Get a Wallet, and Leave instead). Wardrobe and Shop open the same character window on their tab; your profile is still one tap away on the top-bar identicon.

**[NEW] The Shaper, with a way back** — Visit **The Shaper** to preview cosmetics in the world, then **Leave the Shaper** (from the Player Menu or the on-screen button) to return to exactly the room — and roughly the spot — you came from.

**[CHANGE] Room names** — The central home room is now **Hub** (where you spawn and resume). The open communal build room is **Commons**.

**[CHANGE] Return to Hub** — The top-bar escape control is labeled **Return to Hub** (same action as before).

**[NEW] Wardrobe paper doll** — Open your profile to equip passive cosmetics (aura, nameplate, chat bubble, trail) from a character-style layout. Tap a slot to browse what you own and what’s in the shop, preview on your avatar, then **Equip** or **Buy**.

**[NEW] Shop tab** — The character window’s **Shop** tab shows up to **5 featured cosmetics for the day** (the same picks for everyone), with **Buy** on each card — or **Equip** if you already own it. Deployables you own are marked “use from Action Wheel → Items.” A **Go to The Shaper** link sits below to try looks on in-world.

**[NEW] Other players’ profiles** — See their equipped passives and owned deployables (read-only); deployables are still used from the Action Wheel → Items.

**[CHANGE] Wardrobe preview** — Profile doll center is a live WebGL view (avatar on a floor tile, isometric camera) instead of a flat identicon chip; updates with **Wardrobe Preview** and equipped passives.

**[CHANGE]** Closing your profile cancels any try-on preview that wasn’t equipped.

**[CHANGE] Softer Action Wheel** — Right-click (or long-press) your avatar and the wheel now has rounded corners and lighter dividers. Each option shows just its icon; hover one and its name appears above the wheel, and the menu you're in shows its name below. On touch, tap once to see an option's name, tap again to use it (Close/Back still work in a single tap).

**[NEW] Achievements** — Your profile now has an **Achievements** tab at the bottom (yours and other players'), showing total achievement points (AP) and recent unlocks. On your own profile, **View all progress** opens the full list with goals and progress bars. Complete milestones to earn AP; some unlock exclusive trails. A celebration modal pops up the moment you earn one — showing the achievement, its AP, and any reward — and queues up so back-to-back unlocks each get their moment.
