# Public patch notes — players (`UNRELEASED`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[FIX]** **Expired account Re-login** — pick a saved account that shows **Expired**, tap **Re-login**, and the full **Terms / Privacy** checkbox appears where the expiry line was. Check the box and tap **Re-login** again to sign in with your wallet (the box stays checked; no extra error pop-up).
- **[FIX]** **Stacked cubes** — when cubes sit on top of each other, the lower block’s color should read correctly at the seam instead of the upper one bleeding through.
- **[FIX]** **Rotated cubes** — rotated plain cubes render and outline correctly again when placed on a stack.
- **[NEW]** **Prefab library** — in build mode, open **Library** on the prefab strip to choose which saved designs appear in your dock (checkmarks, size filter). Favorites are remembered per wallet on this device.
- **[NEW]** **Prefab create & place** — **Create** saves a design from a floor selection; **Place** works in any room where you can place objects (not only your wallet room). Publishing is always free (no price field).
- **[NEW]** **Walk-through in the build dock** — with an object selected, use the eye control next to rotate/delete to switch between solid collision and walk-through (no need to open Advanced).
- **[FIX]** **Prefabs on raised floor** — designs can be placed on extra-floor tiles, not only the base grid.
- **[FIX]** **Prefab ghost** — closing build mode clears a leftover placement preview.
- **[FIX]** **Walk-through toggle** — the dock eye button no longer flips back immediately or disagrees with the object in the world.
