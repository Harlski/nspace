# Public patch notes — players (`UNRELEASED`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [NEW] **Header ticker:** After you have seen every announcement line once, the strip fades out and stays tucked away for a while unless the lines change—so you are not stuck re-reading the same loop.
- [FIX] **Login screen:** Clearer “Enter game” flow and wallet hint; community links sit below without crowding the main action.
- [FIX] **NIM claim bar:** If you walk away from the block you meant to claim, the bar fades out on its own instead of hanging forever.
- [FIX] **Billboards:** Edit / Move / Delete use the same small pill style as elsewhere; oversized invisible tap areas on those buttons are gone.
- [PERF] **Smoother idle rooms:** When nothing is moving or changing on screen, the game stops redrawing the same 3D scene every frame and wakes rendering back up when players move, you point/click, or the room changes.
- [NEW] **Patch notes screen:** Choose which release to read from a dropdown (like choosing Brief vs Players); one release at a time so the page is easier to scan.
