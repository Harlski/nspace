# Public patch notes — brief (`UNRELEASED`)

**Audience:** widest reach — social posts, in-app one-liner, “what changed at a glance.”  
**Depth:** minimal; no jargon; link to longer tiers if published.

---

The top announcement strip can fade out after you have read every line once, then stay quiet for a bit unless the text changes. Login and the NIM reward strip are clearer, billboard actions match the small pill buttons, and taps line up with what you see. Idle rooms also redraw less aggressively, reducing WebGL frame stalls while you stand still. In **Patch notes**, you pick the release and audience depth from the same style of menu instead of opening a long list of panels.
