# Public patch notes — developers (`UNRELEASED`)

**Audience:** contributors and integrators — APIs, WS messages, module boundaries, notable refactors.  
**Depth:** summarized technical changelog; not a full file list (that stays in [../reasons.md](../reasons.md)).

---

### World Cup soccer (seasonal, feature-flagged)

First **server-simulated dynamic non-player object**. Isolated under `server/src/worldcup/` and `client/src/worldcup/`; core hooks are minimal and greppable by `worldcup`. See [docs/THE-LARGER-SYSTEM.md](../../../docs/THE-LARGER-SYSTEM.md) ("Server-simulated dynamic objects") and `docs/reasons/reason_615240.md`.

- [NEW] **Server modules:** `worldcup/config.ts` (flag, field bounds, goal zones, physics tuning), `ballPhysics.ts` (pure, unit-tested step/kick/bounce/`detectGoal`), `ballStore.ts` (per-room balls + placed-ball persistence in `worldcup-balls.json`), `ballTick.ts` (per-room simulation, proximity kick, throttled broadcast), `scoreStore.ts` (`worldcup-scores.json`: country tally + per-player goals + pending-attribution for scorers without a country yet).
- [NEW] **Authority/tick:** ground-only 2D physics integrated into the existing `rooms.ts` 20 Hz room tick (`tickRoomBalls`). Kicks are **server-detected** from player heading/proximity — there is no client `kick` intent.
- [NEW] **WS messages.** Server→client: `ballState` (`{roomId, balls:[{id,x,z,vx,vz}]}`), `goalScored`, `worldcupLeaderboard`; `welcome` gains `balls`, `worldcupSelfCountry`, `worldcupTopCountries` (field room only). Client→server: `setCountry` (`{code}` ISO 3166-1 alpha-2), `placeBall` (`{x,z}`), `removeBall` (`{ballId}`). Helpers in `client/src/net/ws.ts`: `sendSetCountry`, `sendPlaceBall`, `sendRemoveBall`.
- [NEW] **Field room** registered in `roomLayouts.ts` (server + client parity) only when the flag is on: bounds, hub↔field door pair, two goal zones (`west`/`east`, no teams — any goal credits the last kicker within `LAST_KICKER_WINDOW_MS`).
- [NEW] **Client render** (`Game.ts`): per-ball sphere meshes lerped like remote avatars + goal visuals on the field. Country picker (`worldcup/countryPickerModal.ts`, searchable ISO list, auto-opens on first uncredited goal), field scoreboard HUD (`worldcup/scoreboard.ts`).
- [NEW] **Build-dock "Ball" prop** (Props tab) is an isolated standalone placement mode in `hud.ts` (`isWorldcupBallModeActive()`), kept off the core build-tool union so it deletes cleanly. `main.ts` intercepts placement clicks before block placement. Server caps placed balls at `MAX_PLACED_BALLS_PER_ROOM` and blocks placement where building is disallowed (field/canvas/pixel).
- [NEW] **API:** `GET /api/worldcup/leaderboard` (mirrors the `/api/canvas/leaderboard` pattern; 404 when the flag is off).
- [NEW] **Scoring integrity:** only the official field room counts toward the global tally; per-goal country attribution is immutable; NPCs are excluded; goal-reset cooldown + last-kicker window guard against double-counting.
- [NEW] **Pitch visuals** (client-only) in `client/src/worldcup/fieldVisuals.ts`: procedural `CanvasTexture` soccer ball (applied in `makeWorldcupBallMesh`), grass + line-markings pitch surface overlay, goal netting panels, and tiered stadium stands - all built/disposed by `syncWorldcupGoals`, `raycast`-disabled + `skipBlockPickAndBounds` so they never affect floor picking or collision.
- [CHANGE] **Free movement on the pitch** (field room only): `moveTo` for `FIELD_ROOM_ID` bypasses `snapToTile`/`pathfindTerrain` and sets a single clamped float waypoint, so `advanceAlongPathHuman` walks a straight line at any angle and `player.vx/vz` (hence `kickFromPlayer` heading) can point any direction. The post-arrival grid drift-snap is skipped for the field. Client sends the raw float floor-hit (`pickFloorRaw`) and draws a straight 2-point preview; doors still use the snapped standing-tile check. First per-room non-pathfinding movement variant - see [docs/THE-LARGER-SYSTEM.md](../../../docs/THE-LARGER-SYSTEM.md).
- [CHANGE] **Goal-line detection** (`ballPhysics.ts`): `detectGoal` now requires the ball center to **cross the goal line** (`west`: `x ≤ minX-0.5`; `east`: `x ≥ maxX+0.5`) within the mouth, instead of entering a band in front of it. `stepBall` gained optional `goals`/`goalDepth` args: the end-line wall now has a radius-inset **opening at the mouth** (ball passes through) and a **net box** behind it (back wall at `±GOAL_DEPTH`, posts bound z) so the ball is contained, not reflected, at the goal and can never run away during the post-goal cooldown. `ballTick` passes `FIELD_GOALS`/`GOAL_DEPTH` (field room only). New config `GOAL_DEPTH = 1.2` (matches client net depth).
- [FIX] **Stadium gaps closed** (`fieldVisuals.ts`): added per-tier corner posts so the side stands meet (no outer-corner notch), and a new `buildStadiumGround()` apron plane (below the pitch surface, extends well past the stands) so the pitch↔stand gap and the surround no longer show the sky background on camera rotate.
- [NEW] **Cheering crowd** (client-only) `WorldcupCrowd` in `fieldVisuals.ts`: billboarded **Nimiq identicon** sprites (drawn from a small shared pool of async-loaded `loadIdenticonTexture` faces) seated on the shared `STADIUM` tier layout, an emoji-bubble sprite pool, idle bob, ambient synchronized "wave" cheers, and a goal eruption. Created/disposed alongside the goals in `syncWorldcupGoals`, animated from the render tick, and triggered by `game.worldcupCrowdCheer()` from the `goalScored` handler in `main.ts`. All `raycast`-disabled + `skipBlockPickAndBounds`.
- [CHANGE] **Pitch cleared of NPC wanderers:** `roomAllowsFakePlayers` now excludes `FIELD_ROOM_ID` when the flag is on (like Pixel), so server fake-players no longer spawn on the pitch; the crowd lives in the (client-only) stands instead.
- [CHANGE] **Daily (UTC) scoring + history** in `scoreStore.ts`: goals bucket per UTC day (`today`), completed days archive to `history`, and the most recent winning day is kept as `prevWinner`. `recordGoal`/`setCountry`/`getTopCountries`/`getLeaderboard` all call `rolloverIfNeeded()` (forward-only, by `utcDayKey`); country *choices* (`profiles`) persist across resets, only goal counts reset. Legacy cumulative files migrate to a `0000-legacy` history entry on load. New exports: `rolloverIfNeeded`, `getPreviousDayWinner`, `getHistory`, `utcDayKey`; `getLeaderboard` now also returns `{ day, previousWinner, history }`.
- [NEW] **Daily-reset broadcast + crowd flag.** `rooms.ts` calls `worldcupCheckDailyReset(now)` on the tick; at the UTC boundary it pushes a `worldcupLeaderboard` (cleared `topCountries`, new `prevWinnerCountry`, `dailyReset:true`) to field-room clients. `welcome` gains `worldcupPrevWinnerCountry`; `worldcupLeaderboard` gains `prevWinnerCountry` + `dailyReset`. Client wires the previous winner into `WorldcupCrowd.setFlag()` (held-up flag banners + flag-biased cheer emoji) and a "Yesterday's champion" line + "resets 00:00 UTC" note on the scoreboard. `GET /api/worldcup/leaderboard` now includes `day`, `previousWinner`, and `history`.
- [NEW] **Goalies** (`worldcup/goalie.ts`, pure + unit-tested): a server-controlled keeper defends every goal in the field **and** every Match Pitch — lateral ball-tracking with reaction lag + capped move-speed, A/B `WORLDCUP_GOALIE_MODE` (`kicker` injected into the proximity-kick path / `blocker` circular collider in `stepBall`). Stepped on the room tick, broadcast as throttled `goalieState`. Client draws it as a fixed **House Keeper** identicon billboard + keeper ring (one stable seed for every goal).
- [NEW] **Goal rewards, Free Play Field only** (`worldcup/goalReward.ts`, pure `evaluateGoalReward` + per-UTC-day store `worldcup-goal-rewards.json`): scoring queues a NIM payout via the existing `enqueueNimPayout` queue under layered guards — deterministic idempotent `claimId`, per-wallet daily cap, global daily budget, **Contested** ≥2-player requirement. See `worldcup/adr/0002` + `docs/reasons/reason_562094.md`.
- [CHANGE] **Goal credit reversal (own-goal rule).** `Ball` gains `lastRealKickerAddress`/`lastRealKickAtMs` — the last *human* kicker, never overwritten by a Goalie touch (`ballTick.ts`). Goal credit now uses it, so a deflection/own-goal off the keeper still credits the attacker for the leaderboard **and** the reward; the blocker-mode sentinel-zeroing was removed. Reverses PRD Story 26; see `docs/reasons/reason_884213.md`.
- [NEW] **1v1 Match loop** (`worldcup/match.ts` reducer driven in `rooms.ts`; geometry in `worldcup/matchPitch.ts`): per-conn `challengeOpen`/`challengeRaisedAtMs`/`matchId`; WS in `setChallenge {active}` / `acceptChallenge {targetAddress}` / `leaveMatch`; WS out `matchState {matchId,scoreA,scoreB,phase,remainingMs,aAddress,bAddress}` / `matchEnded {outcome,...}` + `challengeOpen` on broadcast player state. Accepting spins up an ephemeral **Match Pitch** (`MATCH_PITCH_PREFIX`, field bounds reused, never persisted — ADR 0001), snapshots origins, teleports both, runs the reducer (regulation → golden goal → outcome), returns both home after `WORLDCUP_MATCH_RESULT_LINGER_MS`, then tears the empty room down; disconnect/leave forfeits. Match goals score via `scoringSideForGoal` (own goals credit the opponent) and never pay NIM. Helpers `sendSetChallenge`/`sendAcceptChallenge`/`sendLeaveMatch` in `client/src/net/ws.ts`; client Match HUD `client/src/worldcup/matchHud.ts`; Action Wheel **Open to 1v1** toggle + other-player **Accept 1v1** row in `ui/hud.ts`; floating challenge bubble in `Game.ts`.
- [NEW] **Player country on the wire.** `matchState`/`matchEnded` carry `aCountry`/`bCountry`; field-like player snapshots carry `worldcupCountry` (`PlayerState`, server `playerToOutState`, `setCountry` triggers `broadcastRoomStateFull`). Consumed by the scoreboard bar and crowd split.
- [NEW] **Kickoff countdown** (`rooms.ts`): `acceptChallenge` no longer teleports — it registers a `WorldcupPendingMatch` (per-conn `pendingMatchId`), broadcasts a 🤝 handshake bubble + a new `matchCountdown {durationMs, opponentAddress, opponentCountry, selfCountry}` message to the origin room, and `worldcupTickPending` teleports both after `WORLDCUP_MATCH_COUNTDOWN_MS` (config `MATCH.countdownMs`); a disconnect/leave during the countdown aborts. Client overlay `client/src/worldcup/matchCountdown.ts`.
- [NEW] **Spectating** (implements issue 80): per-conn `spectatingMatchId` (distinct from `matchId`, so a spectator disconnect never forfeits); WS in `requestSpectate {matchId}`, WS out `matchPortalSpawn`/`matchPortalRemove` + `welcome.worldcupPortals`. `requestSpectate` validates, snapshots origin, checks `WORLDCUP_MATCH_SPECTATOR_CAP` (portal "full" past the cap), teleports to a fixed `worldcupSpectatorSeat`; spectators are excluded from `ballPlayers` and the `moveTo` handler, auto-returned on match end, and the portal is removed on end. `sendRequestSpectate` in `net/ws.ts`.
- [CHANGE] **Spectate via walk-on + intent (no teleport-on-click).** Removed `Game.tryWorldcupPortalClickAt` (and the `setSpectatePortalHandler` raycast path); a click on the portal now falls through to normal tap-to-move. Each portal entry stores its snapped footprint `tileX/tileZ` + `full`; new `Game.getStandingSpectatePortal()` returns `{matchId, full}` when the player stands on that tile (mirrors `getStandingDoor`). `main.ts` `syncPortalEnterButton` raises a "Watch" / "Stands full" pill via the existing `portalAction`/`onPortalEnter` door path; pressing it (not full) calls `beginRoomTransition(matchId)` then `sendRequestSpectate`.
- [CHANGE] **Loading screen into Match Pitches.** `loadingLabelForTargetRoom` returns "Entering the match..." for `wc-match-` rooms; `beginRoomTransition(roomId, labelOverride?)` gained an optional label. Spectators trigger it on the Watch intent; players trigger it via `worldcupMatchEnterTimer` scheduled on the `matchCountdown` handler (`setTimeout(durationMs)` → `beginRoomTransition("", "Entering the match...")`), cleared in the `welcome` handler and on socket close so a cancelled match can't strand a black screen. The pitch `welcome` hides the loading screen as usual. (Return-to-origin at match end intentionally has no loading screen — it would cover the lingering result banner.)
- [CHANGE] **Flat scoreboard bar** (`worldcup/matchHud.ts`, rewritten): horizontal `{flag}{identicon} scoreA · clock · scoreB {identicon}{flag}` bar positioned under `.hud-top-wrap` via `ResizeObserver`; Leave button at the right edge (label flips to "Stop watching" for spectators); inline result on end.
- [CHANGE] **Worldcup HUD bounded to the letterbox.** `WorldcupScoreboard` and `WorldcupMatchHud` constructors now take a `parent` element and mount with `position:absolute` into the `.letterbox` game area (passed from `main.ts` as `canvasHost.parentElement`) instead of `position:fixed` on `document.body`, so they no longer spill into the black letterbox bars; the centred goal banner stays on `document.body`. The scoreboard also gained **tap-to-collapse/expand** (`setCollapsed`, persisted in `localStorage` `wc_scoreboard_collapsed`): collapsed shows a compact `⚽ {leader flag} {goals}` summary and hides the list/subtitle/champion line; header toggle, flag-pick button `stopPropagation`s. Responsive width (`min(220px,46vw)`).
- [CHANGE] **Goalie tuned beatable + new knobs** (`worldcup/goalie.ts`, `config.ts`, `rooms.ts`). `goalieZRange(goal, radius, coverage=1)` now scales the radius-inset patrol half-width by a coverage fraction (centred on the mouth) so `<1` leaves a fixed corner gap; `GoalieTuning.coverage` threads through `stepGoalie`. New config `GOALIE.coverage` (`WORLDCUP_GOALIE_COVERAGE_PCT`, default 60) and `GOALIE.kickReach` (`WORLDCUP_GOALIE_KICK_REACH_TENTHS`, default 0.3); `moveSpeed` 4→2.5 (now `envFloat`), `reactionMs` 180→260. `TickPlayer` gained optional `kickReach` (used in `ballTick`'s `canKick` gate, default `BALL_KICK.reach`); `rooms.ts` injects the keeper kicker with `kickReach: GOALIE.kickReach`. Tests extended in `worldcup-goalie.test.ts` (coverage geometry) and `worldcup-ballPhysics.test.ts` (per-kicker reach via `tickRoomBalls`).
- [NEW] **Crowd allegiance** (`worldcup/fieldVisuals.ts`): `WorldcupCrowd` seats gain `side`/`flag`; `setSideFlags` (1v1 split by pitch side) and `setRosterFlags` (Free Play live roster, even split over distinct present countries) with champion-flag fallback (`setFlag`). Wired from `main.ts` via match country fields (1v1) and `worldcupCountry` roster (free play); `Game.ts` `applyWorldcupCrowdFlags`.
- [NEW/CHANGE] **Floating touch joystick** (`worldcup/joystick.ts`, soccer rooms only): on `(pointer: coarse)` / Nimiq Pay hosts. The joystick is now a **visual-only** widget (`WorldcupJoystickView`: `showAt`/`moveThumbTo`/`hide`) with no own listeners or fixed position; **`Game.ts` owns the gesture** on its existing single-finger pitch pointer pipeline. A quick stationary tap still walks (tap-to-move on `pointerup`); a drag past `WORLDCUP_STICK_ENGAGE_PX` (~14px) promotes the deferred walk into the stick, anchored at the thumb-down point (`beginWorldcupStick`), with 140ms throttled `worldcupJoystickMove` emits (camera-relative ground basis → far clamped point) and `worldcupJoystickStop` on release. Release sends a dedicated **stop** intent (`sendStopMove` → `moveTo {stop:true}`) that the server applies un-rate-limited (clears the path queue), so the player halts the instant the finger lifts instead of gliding toward the last far target (a plain stop `moveTo` would be dropped by the 120ms move rate limit). The steering finger is locked: a second finger is ignored (no two-finger camera/zoom while steering). `Game.setWorldcupJoystickView` / `setWorldcupJoystickEnabled` (pitch + touch host + not spectating) wired from `main.ts`. On the pitch a thumb-down on any ground hit (incl. the outfield margin) arms the deferred walk so both tap-to-move and the stick work from anywhere.
- [CHANGE] **Click-toward-tap** (`Game.ts`): `resolveWalkNavigationGoalAt` falls back to `nearestWalkableTileToWorld` (expanding-ring search) when the click isn't on a walkable tile, so off-tile grid clicks still move.
- [CHANGE] **Outfield margin** (config `FIELD_OUTFIELD_MARGIN` / `WORLDCUP_FIELD_OUTFIELD_MARGIN`, default 1.0): `rooms.ts` `worldcupMoveClampBounds` widens the player clamp by the margin for field-like rooms in `advanceAlongPathHuman` + the field `moveTo` branch. Ball collision walls stay at the true bounds.
- [CHANGE] **Tap/click-to-move clamps to the wall** (`Game.ts` `tryFieldFreeWalkAt`): the click target is now clamped to `WORLDCUP_FIELD_BOUNDS` (the field boundary) instead of `worldcupFieldMoveBounds` (the outfield margin), so a tap past the touchline walks to the nearest point on the wall and matches the path preview (which already clamped to the bounds). The outfield margin stays reachable via the joystick (`worldcupJoystickMove` still clamps to `worldcupFieldMoveBounds`) and the server clamp.
- [NEW] **1v1 goal banner + post-goal kickoff reset** (`worldcup/config.ts`, `rooms.ts`, `worldcup/matchHud.ts`, `Game.ts`). `worldcupHandleMatchGoal` now broadcasts a new `matchGoal {matchId, side, scoreA, scoreB, country, kickoffMs}` after a Match Pitch goal (previously goals only updated `matchState`, so 1v1s had no goal feedback). The client erupts the crowd and flashes a centre-screen "GOAL!" banner (`WorldcupMatchHud.flashGoal`). When the goal doesn't end the Match (`kickoffMs = MATCH.goalResetMs`, env `WORLDCUP_MATCH_GOAL_RESET_MS`, default 5000), `worldcupKickoffReset` snaps both participants to `matchSpawn`, re-centres the ball (`spawnMatchBall`) + keepers, and sets `WorldcupMatchRuntime.kickoffUntilMs`; while that's in the future the `moveTo` handler rejects participant moves and `worldcupTickMatches` pauses the Match clock (skips the `tick` reduce, keeps `lastTickMs` current). Client gates input with `Game.setWorldcupMoveLocked` (suppresses tap-to-move + joystick + preview), driven from the `matchGoal` handler with a `setTimeout(kickoffMs)` unlock and cleared on `matchEnded`/`welcome`. The match-ending goal sends `kickoffMs: 0` (banner only; `matchEnded` shows the result).
- [NEW/CHANGE] **Match Pitch view polish** (client-only, no server/WS changes; driven from the `matchState` handler in `main.ts`). New `Game` methods: `setWorldcupMatchParticipants(a,b)` records the two players so any other avatar in a Match Pitch is treated as a Spectator; `setWorldcupSpectatorView(active)` frames the whole pitch (locks zoom at `effectiveZoomMax()` and centers `updateCameraFollow` on the pitch center instead of the stand seat); `setWorldcupAttackGoal(side)` shows a bobbing cyan arrow (`client/src/worldcup/goalArrow.ts`, `WorldcupGoalArrow`) over the attacked net (side a = east, side b = west); `setWorldcupMatchOrientation(side)` defaults `cameraOrbitYawRad` to `π` for side a (west/NW spawn, applied once on entry, still user-re-orbitable) and `0` for side b. Spectator avatars are visually seated on the stand front row via `frontRowStandSeat()` (new export in `fieldVisuals.ts`) applied after the per-frame position lerp. All teardown happens in `main.ts` on leaving a Match Pitch; the goal arrow is also dropped in `syncWorldcupGoals`.
- [OPS] **Deprecation:** `WORLDCUP_ENABLED` / `VITE_WORLDCUP_ENABLED` off ⇒ no door/ball/goals/UI (and field movement reverts to standard grid pathfinding). Full removal = delete the two `worldcup/` source trees (incl. `fieldVisuals.ts`, `goalArrow.ts`), `worldcup/issues/`, and the `worldcup`-tagged hook lines; retain `worldcup-scores.json` as the archive.
