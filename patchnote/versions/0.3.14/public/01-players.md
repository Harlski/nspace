# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.14`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[NEW]** **Build** uses a **bottom dock**: **Terrain**, **Props**, and **Buildings** tabs, **tool previews**, and a **live 3D preview** for what you place or select.
- **[NEW]** **Room** editing (when allowed): **Floor** tab for expanding floor, **Room settings** for **background color** (swatch opens a color wheel).
- **[NEW]** **Teleporters** in the **same room** can be placed as a **linked pair**—removing one removes both.
- **[CHANGE]** **Objects** vs **Room** is chosen on the **dock tab row** (on phones, a **tap menu** instead of the system picker).
- **[CHANGE]** **Room background color** sits in the **right-hand build options** panel with other parameters, not in the tool list.
- **[FIX]** Closing build with **×** (including from **Room settings**) no longer leaves you stuck in **Room** when you turn **Build** on again—it **reopens on Objects**.
- **[CHANGE]** Less **tutorial text** on the build bar; controls stay compact.
