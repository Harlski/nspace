# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.12`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[NEW]** When you **reconnect** or **enter a room**, the chat panel can show **recent messages from that room** so the conversation does not feel like it vanished (only messages from a short window; very old lines still roll off as before).
- **[NEW]** **Signposts** (message posts in the world) show a **small document icon** above them with a gentle bounce so they are easier to spot from a distance or without hovering — visibility eases when you are far away, **gets a bit stronger when you read the sign’s tooltip**, and the icon **stays hidden** when another block sits on top or sits in front of it from your camera. The marker hides while you are in **build mode**.
- **[NEW]** If the world is about to go offline for a **planned restart**, a **short countdown strip** can appear at the top; if you disconnect then, the status line explains it is likely **maintenance** rather than a random network fault.
