# Public patch notes — players (`0.4.0`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

### World Cup soccer (seasonal)

- [NEW] **Soccer Field room.** A new door in the hub leads to a standalone pitch with two goals. No teams, no match clock — just turn up and play.
- [NEW] **Kick by running.** Walk into the ball and it launches in your direction. The faster you move, the harder it goes. There's no button to press.
- [NEW] **Score for your country.** Put the ball in either goal and it counts. The first time you score, you'll be asked to pick your country from a searchable flag list. Your pick is remembered and applied to future goals; you can change it anytime from the flag button on the scoreboard.
- [NEW] **Live scoreboard.** A panel on the field shows the top countries and updates the moment anyone scores. Past goals always keep the country they were scored under, even if you switch flags later.
- [NEW] **Bring a ball anywhere.** In rooms where you can build, a new **Ball** prop in the build dock (Props tab) lets you place a kickaround ball so people can play outside the field too. Only goals on the official Soccer Field count toward the global tally.
- [NEW] **A proper pitch.** The Soccer Field now looks the part: a striped grass surface with full line markings, goals with netting, and stadium stands around the ground. The ball looks like a real soccer ball and tumbles as it rolls.
- [CHANGE] **Move any direction on the pitch.** On the Soccer Field you now walk in a straight line to wherever you click - any angle - instead of being snapped to the grid. That means you can finally shoot the ball diagonally, not just straight up/down/left/right. (Other rooms are unchanged.)
- [NEW] **A cheering crowd.** The stands are now packed with spectators — each one a Nimiq identicon — who bob along, throw up emoji, and ripple through the occasional wave. When anyone scores, the whole stadium leaps up and erupts in celebration. The pitch itself is kept clear for players — the wandering NPCs stay in the stands.
- [CHANGE] **Goals must actually go in.** A shot now only counts once the ball crosses the goal line *between the posts* — no more scoring from in front of the goal. The end line is open at the mouth so the ball rolls into the net, and balls hit wide of the posts bounce back off the end line.
- [FIX] **No more gaps around the stadium.** Closed the holes at the stand corners and filled the strip between the pitch and the seats (and the area around the ground) so rotating the camera no longer shows the sky through the stadium.
- [NEW] **Daily standings + a champion crowd.** The scoreboard now runs day by day and resets at midnight UTC, so every day is a fresh race. The previous day's winning country becomes the crowd's team — spectators wave that nation's flag and chant for them until someone takes the crown the next day. Past days are kept, and the scoreboard shows yesterday's champion.
- [NEW] **Goalkeepers in every net.** A keeper now guards each goal and tracks the ball, so scoring takes a bit of skill — aim wide of it or shoot hard. The keeper is its own little "house" identicon with a ring at its feet, so you'll never confuse it with another player.
- [NEW] **Score NIM on the field.** Every goal you score on the public Soccer Field now earns a small NIM reward (sent automatically), as long as there's at least one other player around. There are daily limits to keep things fair. If a goal deflects in off the keeper — even an own goal it fumbles — the last player who actually kicked the ball gets the credit and the reward.
- [NEW] **Challenge someone to a 1v1.** Open the radial menu on yourself and pick **Open to 1v1** to raise a challenge — a little "⚔ 1v1?" badge floats above you. Anyone nearby can right-click you and **Accept**. You're both whisked onto a private pitch with keepers and a match clock; first to lead when time runs out wins, with sudden-death golden goal if it's tied. A match HUD shows the clock, the score, and the result, and there's a **Leave match** button any time. (Matches are just for fun — no NIM, no leaderboard.)
- [NEW] **A proper kickoff.** Accepting a challenge no longer teleports you instantly. Both players shake hands (🤝) and a "Match starting in 3… 2… 1…" overlay counts you in before you drop onto the pitch — so nobody starts mid-stride. If someone bails during the countdown, the match is called off.
- [NEW] **Watch matches from the stands.** When a 1v1 kicks off, a "{you} vs {them}" portal appears where it started. Tap it to drop into the stands and watch live — pick your seat, soak up the atmosphere, and you'll be sent home automatically when the match ends. (Stands have a limit; the portal shows "full" once it's packed.) Watching is hands-off: spectators can't touch the ball.
- [NEW] **Pick a side in the crowd.** The stadium now picks teams: in a 1v1, each half of the crowd waves one player's flag. On the public field, the stands wave the flags of whoever's actually playing right now.
- [NEW] **A cleaner scoreboard.** The match score now sits in a slim bar tucked neatly under the top bar — both flags, both identicons, the score, and the clock in one glance — instead of floating over everything.
- [NEW] **Thumb-stick on mobile.** On phones (and in Nimiq Pay) a joystick lets you run around the soccer pitch with your thumb — and it now **pops up wherever you press down**, so you don't have to reach for a fixed spot. Just press and drag to steer; lift to stop. A quick tap still walks you there, so use whichever feels better.
- [CHANGE] **Tap anywhere to walk.** On the grid, tapping a spot you can't stand on (a wall, off the edge) now walks you to the nearest spot you *can* stand on, instead of doing nothing.
- [CHANGE] **Step behind the ball.** You can now walk a little past the pitch edges, so when the ball is pinned against a wall you can get behind it and play it back instead of just bouncing it off.
- [NEW] **GOAL! in 1v1 matches.** Scoring in a 1v1 now lights up the screen: the crowd erupts and a big "GOAL!" flashes with the scorer's flag and the new score — no more silent goals.
- [CHANGE] **Kickoff after every goal.** Once a goal goes in, both players are sent back to their starting spots and held for a short "Kickoff in 3… 2… 1…" countdown before play restarts — a clean reset instead of carrying on from wherever you were. (The match clock pauses during the countdown.)
- [CHANGE] **Tap-to-move stops at the wall.** On the pitch, tapping outside the field now walks you to the nearest point on the touchline (matching the on-screen path line), instead of drifting a step past it.
- [FIX] **Spectators sit in the stands.** When you watch a 1v1 you're now seated on the front row of the stand instead of being stuck inside the seating.
- [CHANGE] **See the whole pitch while watching.** Spectating now zooms out to frame the entire field and holds it there, so you can follow the play end to end.
- [NEW] **Know which goal is yours.** In a 1v1 a bobbing arrow hovers over the goal you're attacking, so there's never any doubt which net to aim for.
- [CHANGE] **A fair, even pitch.** Both players now start with their view set so they're shooting the same way down the screen — no more one player having an easier-looking goal. You can still rotate the camera however you like.
- [CHANGE] **Scoring is fairer now.** The keeper used to smother almost everything, so the only goals were lucky deflections. Now it guards the middle but leaves the corners open and is a touch slower to react — pick your spot, put pace on it, and a well-placed shot into the corner beats it. A tame roll down the middle still gets saved.
- [CHANGE] **A tidier World Cup scoreboard.** The standings panel now stays neatly inside the game view instead of hanging off into the black border, and you can **tap it to collapse or expand** — shrink it to a small badge showing the current leader when you want the screen clear. It remembers your choice.
- [CHANGE] **Walk up to watch a match.** To spectate a 1v1 you now step onto the "vs" portal and press **Watch**, instead of being teleported the instant you tap it — same as walking through a door. A loading screen plays as you drop into the stands.
- [CHANGE] **Loading screen into matches.** Dropping onto a 1v1 pitch — as a player after the countdown, or as a spectator — now shows a short "Entering the match…" loading screen instead of snapping in abruptly.
