# Public patch notes — players (`0.6.0`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [NEW] **Unlock Pads** — paid crossings that stay solid until you unlock; then they become a low plate you can walk (others still see the locked block). Outside the tutorial, unlock uses on-chain Payment Intent verify. Admins can place and configure pads in build mode.
- [NEW] **Attention Markers** — admins can place a glowing floating V over mine blocks, Unlock Pads, or empty tiles to mark where to look or act (Hover Height, Size, and color).
- [NEW] **No-Walk Floor** — admins can paint floor that still looks solid but blocks walking (red X cues while editing floor).
- [CHANGE] The Nimiq Pay tutorial room is a tall portrait path: mine at the south end, Unlock Pad in the middle, exit teleporter toward the north.
- [CHANGE] The Nimiq Pay tutorial lesson is off unless operators turn it on; until then you start in the Hub. When it is on, new or unfinished Pay wallets enter the Tutorial Room on sign-in.
- [FIX] After mining in the tutorial, stand beside the Unlock Pad for an Unlock control above the tile (opens Nimiq Pay).
- [CHANGE] Room → Floor color picking is a full-spectrum square (light and dark of any color), sized to the tool cards so the dock does not grow.
- [CHANGE] Floor spawn no longer shows a Use room center button in the build dock (click a walkable tile to set Join Spawn).
