# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.23`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[NEW]** **Usernames** — after sign-in you are prompted to choose a display name (letters and numbers, up to 12 characters). A short **loading spinner** appears first, then a simple screen: your **wallet identicon**, **Pick a username**, the name field, and **Defers Remaining** (small text under the field when **Not now** is still available). You can tap **Not now** up to **five times**; after that you must pick a name before entering the world. The prompt also appears when you **Enter** with a **saved wallet** (not only on a fresh sign-in). Offensive and reserved names are rejected. Your **username** appears in the **top bar** beside your identicon (wallet shorthand when you have not set a name yet). Change your name from your **profile** card; after the first pick, changes are limited to once per **24 hours**. If a moderator clears your name, you can choose a new one on your next sign-in (unless you are banned from setting names).
- **[FIX]** If you delete a room or set it to private, that choice now survives server restarts and updates. Previously, deleted rooms could reappear and private rooms could show up in the public list again after a deploy.
- **[CHANGE]** When you create a new room, you need to enter a name first — the form no longer auto-fills a default like “YourWallet's room”. Pick something you want before you create and enter.
- **[NEW]** If you disconnect or close the tab and come back within about **ten minutes**, you rejoin the **same room and spot** where you left off. After longer than that, you spawn in the **chamber** as usual. If that spot is no longer valid (for example the floor tile was removed), you also land in the chamber instead of getting stuck.
- **[NEW]** **Right-click** a line in the chat log for quick actions: **View Profile**, **Report Message** (other players’ messages — opens a report form with the message shown read-only), and **More** → **Copy Message** or **Translate** (Google Translate, same as before).
- **[NEW]** **Feedback** (your **profile** card, bottom-left) — submit **bugs**, **feature requests**, or **suggestions**. **New** and **My feedback** tabs sit centered at the top of the overlay; **Send** / **Reply** is on the left, **Close** on the right. Open **My feedback** to see your tickets, read team replies, and add follow-ups. A **red dot** on your **top-bar profile** (identicon) and on the **Feedback** button when you have an unread team reply. If a ticket is rewarded, the **NIM** amount appears on that ticket.
