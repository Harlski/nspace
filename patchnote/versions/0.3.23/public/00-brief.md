# Public patch notes — brief (`0.3.23`)

**Audience:** widest reach — social posts, in-app one-liner, “what changed at a glance.”  
**Depth:** minimal; no jargon; link to longer tiers if published.

---

- **[NEW]** **Usernames** — pick a name when you sign in; your username shows in the top bar next to your identicon.
- **[FIX]** Deleted and private player rooms stay that way after a server update.
- **[CHANGE]** You must choose a room name when creating a new room.
- **[NEW]** Reconnect within about ten minutes and you return to where you left off; longer breaks send you back to the chamber.
- **[NEW]** Right-click chat messages for profile, report, copy, or translate.
- **[NEW]** Feedback tickets — submit bugs and ideas, track replies in-game; a dot on your top-bar profile when the team has replied; admins manage at `/admin/feedback`.
