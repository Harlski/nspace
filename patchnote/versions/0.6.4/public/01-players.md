# Public patch notes — players (`0.6.4`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [NEW] Completing each tutorial step plays a short cinematic title: **You just earned NIM**, **You just spent NIM**, then **Welcome to Nimiq Space** when you reach the Hub (including via the Exit Teleporter).
- [NEW] Tutorial progress is a slim **Mine → Pay → Exit** track that fills as you complete each step.
- [FIX] Tutorial **Unlock** now opens Nimiq Hub to pay on desktop when you are not in the Nimiq Pay app.
- [NEW] Before paying to unlock, a confirmation popup shows how much NIM you are about to send.
- [CHANGE] The tutorial control now reads **Unlock** (was “Unlock Pad”). Reset for testing stays in the player menu.
- [PERF] Occasional lag spikes while people are mining / claiming blocks should be much rarer.
