# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.5`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[NEW]** **Wallet room entry spawn** — set a landing spot on the map for people who join your room (build mode).
- **[CHANGE]** **Gates** — clearer green/red hints while placing or moving a gate; reposition preview shows a ghost and keeps the door readable while you drag; **R** rotates the exit side when the gate tool is active; optional **colors** on new gates; **double-click** a gate (when you are next to it) tries to open it if you are allowed, with a short on-screen message if not.
- **[CHANGE]** **Who can open a gate** — manage access from the object panel (**Permissions**) instead of digging through old controls; hub gates can be opened more predictably when the space allows walking through.
- **[FIX]** **Movement on and around gates** — fewer awkward snaps or stuck feelings when a gate closes or when pathfinding crosses gate tiles.
- **[NEW]** **Header line** — occasional scrolling streak labels and rotating short messages at the top of the screen (when the server provides them).
- **[CHANGE]** **About / version** — the version shown in the client matches the release you are on, not a hard-coded older label.
