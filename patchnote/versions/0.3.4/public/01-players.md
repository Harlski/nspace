# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.4`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **Gates:** clearer green/red hints while placing or moving a gate; a slimmer door panel; **R** rotates the exit when the gate tool is active; double-click a gate in walk mode (when you are allowed) opens it; if opening would trap you on unwalkable terrain, you get a short on-screen message instead of silent failure.
- **Who can open:** in **wallet rooms**, gate access still follows the owner’s list; in the **hub**, anyone can open a gate so people can move through together while it stays open briefly.
- **Stuck after close:** if you were still on the gate tile when it **closed**, you should no longer end up stuck on top of the door — you snap back to normal floor beside you.
- **Wallet rooms — entry spawn:** room owners can place where new visitors land (pulsing ring in build mode); your last spawn there can be remembered per wallet.
- **Header:** optional streak ticker and rotating announcements (content comes from the live service when configured).
- **Main menu:** the small version text under the logo matches the release you are running.
