# Public patch notes — players (`0.5.6`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[PERF]** **Smoother walking for others** — when the live server has move-order sync enabled, other players’ click-to-walk paths animate along the route instead of stuttering on every server tick. Your own avatar follows the same smooth path in those rooms.
- **[FIX]** **World Cup keepers** — goalie positions now appear correctly when you enter or teleport onto a field, including mid-match joiners, instead of sometimes sitting at the wrong spot until the next update.
- **[NEW]** **Floor eyedropper** — while painting floor tiles, hold **Alt** or tap the eyedropper button in the build dock to sample a color from the room. The picked color loads into your brush.
- **[NEW]** **Exact floor hex color** — tap the hex label under the floor hue ring to enter a precise `#RRGGBB` color.
- **[NEW]** **Play Space sharing** — the private-room invite lobby adds **Copy link** and **Share on X** buttons below the QR code.
- **[NEW]** **Feedback in the player menu** — open **Feedback** from the **⋯** player menu (same tickets as before). A blue dot appears when an admin reply is unread.
