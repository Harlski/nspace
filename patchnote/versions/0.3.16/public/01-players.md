# Public patch notes — players (`0.3.16`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- **[NEW]** **Block and gate colors** — use the **hue ring** in the build dock; **click the center** to type a custom **#RRGGBB** hex (works while placing and while editing a selected block).
- **[NEW]** **Hex** blocks have a **Thickness** control (±, 25–100%); **sphere** blocks have **Size** (±, 25–100%)—same style as height and pyramid base.
- **[NEW]** **Room sky color** — in build mode, **Room → Room settings**, open the background swatch for a **hue wheel** and **hex** field (like block colors).
- **[FIX]** The **placement hex** popover **stays open** while you type; preview updates without dismissing the field.
- **[FIX]** **Thickness / size / height** steppers no longer **jump back** to an old value right after you tap ±.
- **[FIX]** Room background **#000000** reads as **black** (not a red tint); other very gray hex values map to sensible neutrals.
- **[FIX]** The **room stats / debug** box is **off by default**; open **your profile** and **tap your identicon** to show or hide it.
- **[CHANGE]** Preset **recent color** chips are gone—use the ring or hex instead. The main menu no longer shows floating NIM logos behind the buttons.
