# Public patch notes — players (`0.4.5`)

**Audience:** people who play or explore Nimiq Space — features, fixes, and feel; not implementation detail.  
**Depth:** short bullets or short paragraphs; avoid file paths and internal names unless they help (e.g. a renamed control).

---

- [NEW] **Match Result Overlay** — when a 1v1 Match ends, a centre-screen card shows both players’ identicons (the winner stays bright, the loser fades — on a draw, both stay full), a personalized headline (“You win!”, “You lost”, “Draw”, or opponent-left copy), the final score, and a “Returning in N…” countdown. **Leave** (or **Stop watching** if you were spectating) sends you home early; otherwise you’re teleported back when the timer runs out. You can’t move during this screen. The scoreboard bar above keeps the final score.
- [CHANGE] **Join codes are six characters** — wallet rooms and new Play Spaces both use a six-letter/digit code (shown in uppercase). Type it in the Rooms browser’s “Join with code” field; letter case no longer matters for these codes. **Older eight-character Play Space links and codes still work** if you already have one.
- [CHANGE] **Before a Match starts**, the handshake countdown in the origin room is a little longer by default (about ten seconds instead of three), giving both sides more time to get ready.
- [PERF] **Pitch movement feels smoother** on the Free Play Field and in 1v1 — especially when steering with the touch joystick — with less stutter between your input and your avatar.
