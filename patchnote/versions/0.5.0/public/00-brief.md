# Public patch notes — brief (`0.5.0`)

**Audience:** widest reach — social posts, in-app one-liner, “what changed at a glance.”  
**Depth:** minimal; no jargon; link to longer tiers if published.

---

**[NEW]** Player Menu in the bottom-right — tap your identicon for Wardrobe, Shop, Achievements, and Rooms; long-press it for the Action Wheel. Your profile lives on the top-bar identicon.

**[NEW]** Achievements — earn AP, unlock exclusive trails, browse progress in the Achievements window, and celebrate unlocks with a banner plus an in-world trophy pop.

**[NEW]** Wardrobe paper doll — equip and preview cosmetics on a live avatar; Shop tab shows daily featured picks when the shop is open.

**[CHANGE]** Hub and Commons — the central home room is **Hub**; the open build room is **Commons**.
