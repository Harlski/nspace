This policy describes what we collect, why we use it, how long we keep it, whom we disclose it to, and what rights you may exercise. Nimiq Space is primarily an online game/service; usage data is gathered to operate, secure, monetise ethically (if configured), troubleshoot, roadmap, optimise performance, fairness, payouts, replays/moderations, infra capacity planning — overall to improve and maintain the game service. Contact details appear at the bottom of this page.

Operator & contact

Nimiq Space is operated as a community multiplayer service for the Nimiq ecosystem. For access, rectification, erasure, portability, objections, supervisory complaints, or any other privacy-related request, email nimiqspace@gmail.com. We route requests to the responsible operator and respond within reasonable timelines, subject to technical limits (for example, immutable on-chain references cannot be erased from the chain itself).

Data we process

Depending on configuration, categories may include:

  • Wallet addresses / public keys. Used as pseudonymous identifiers and for authentication / payouts.
  • Gameplay & session analytics. Connection times, movements, placements, removals, chats, room IDs, durations, payouts, leaderboard-like streak aggregates, anomaly flags — written to rotating event / telemetry files on operators’ servers unless disabled.
  • Profiles & in-world artefacts. Optional display nicknames/status lines, billboard content, block placements attributable to wallets in persisted world snapshots.
  • Operational & security logs. HTTP paths, IPs, UA strings, JWT validation failures, anomaly triage artefacts, infra metrics, IDS hits, webhook traces, uptime monitors, SMTP relay logs — proportionate retention enforced by playbook.
  • Standalone analytics / payouts / admin SPA beacons. Page-load counters may record anonymous / authorised wallet ids per operator allowlists — see codebase & checklist for specifics.
  • Recorded acknowledgements. Wallet id + accepted published document version + timestamp showing Terms and Privacy Policy were presented and acknowledged when the service requires it.

Purposes & bases

  • Providing gameplay, syncing world state with authority, administering rewards or payments when enabled — contractual necessity.
  • Security, moderation, cheating detection, infra reliability, orders from competent authorities, analytics to improve fairness & uptime — legitimate interests proportionately balanced against fundamental rights unless explicit permission is required in your region.

Recipients

Hosting providers, Nimiq tooling (wallet / indexer / RPC gateways), SMTP/DMARC watchers, alerting vendors, payment processors backing optional payout pathways, subpoena responders, auditors under NDA — processors bound by SCCs/subprocessor lists where applicable. You can ask for an up-to-date summary of subprocessors—or a fuller list—from nimiqspace@gmail.com.

Retention

Operational logs honour rolling windows (often days/weeks/months configurable). Event/session archives purge per operator policy and disk budgets. Backups may temporarily retain erased personal data encrypted until rotations complete. Acknowledgement rows persist until a deletion request is processed or a mandatory preservation period ends.

Your rights

Depending on jurisdiction you may ask for access, rectification of profile fields server-side editors allow, erasure where rules permit (some blockchain-linked pseudonyms cannot mutate chain history itself), portability of machine-readable artefacts we control, or objection to certain processing — email nimiqspace@gmail.com. Complain to your supervisory authority after contacting us unless urgent authority investigations apply during active abuse triage triggering temporary locks.

International transfers

Servers may reside outside your country. Where EEA/UK/Brazil or similar regimes apply, document appropriate transfer tools (for example SCCs, UK IDTA, or regional bridges) together with your advisers so processing stays aligned with obligations in the regions you serve.

Children

Nimiq Space is not directed at young children. If you are below the age of majority where you live, use the service only with a parent or guardian’s permission and oversight—especially where a cryptocurrency wallet or on-chain assets are involved.

Browser storage

Sessions/cache tokens/localStorage flags tied to JWT lifetimes aid convenience; revoke via logout/forgot-device.

Changes

Material updates bump the published document version; reconnecting wallets may prompt re-acknowledgement per server configuration.
