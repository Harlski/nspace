# Unlock Aftermath: crossing or per-wallet Teleporter

**Superseded** by [0012-unlock-pad-crossing-only.md](0012-unlock-pad-crossing-only.md).
Unlock Aftermath is always a per-wallet crossing; Teleporter Aftermath is removed. Tutorial
Path Exit is a separate **Exit Teleporter** north of the Unlock Pad.

---

**Historical:** **Unlock Pad** gained an authored **Unlock Aftermath**: after a wallet
unlocked, that wallet only got either a walkable **crossing** on the tile (default for new
pads) or a **Teleporter** on the same tile (destination Room + Landing Hint required in
Unlock Pad Settings). Locked wallets still saw the solid pad. Teleporter Aftermath was a
per-wallet visual and behavior swap — not a second placed obstacle. The **Tutorial Path**
authored Aftermath = Teleporter → Hub on the Pay-band pad; lesson complete was Entering that
Teleporter. **Tutorial Escape** granted unlock and forced Hub without completion.

That “pad becomes the exit” beat fought walkability and rejoin persistence. Replaced by
crossing-only Unlock Pad + authored Exit Teleporter.
