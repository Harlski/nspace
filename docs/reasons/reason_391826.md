# reason_391826 — Uncap Free Play Field NIM earning; Solo Goal half-rate

## Why THE-LARGER-SYSTEM was updated

Post-launch play showed the layered goal-reward caps (per-wallet daily cap, global daily
budget, and the binary Contested gate that paid nothing when solo) were blocking legitimate
solo practice and capping engagement too aggressively for a seasonal kickaround. Issue
**06-uncap-free-play-nim** relaxed the model while keeping emergency operator knobs.

## How it serves the larger system

- **Solo play still earns, at half rate.** One player in the field gets `floor(draw / 2)`;
  two or more distinct players get the full random draw. The old `not_contested` hard block
  is gone — **Solo Goal** replaces it as a softer anti-farming lever.
- **Caps are off by default, not deleted.** `WORLDCUP_GOAL_REWARD_DAILY_CAP_PER_WALLET=0`
  and `WORLDCUP_GOAL_REWARD_DAILY_BUDGET_NIM=0` mean unlimited in normal ops; operators
  can set positive values as a treasury emergency brake without redeploying logic.
- **Attribution unchanged.** Only the **Last Real Kicker** is paid; goalie-only goals still
  pay nobody. Matches still never pay — the collusion guardrail from
  [reason_562094](reason_562094.md) holds.
- **Same seams.** The pure `evaluateGoalReward` evaluator and per-UTC-day store stay; payout
  still flows through `enqueueNimPayout` with deterministic `claimId` dedupe.
