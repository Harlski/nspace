# reason_884213 — Goal credit survives Goalie deflections; ephemeral 1v1 Match Pitch loop

## Why THE-LARGER-SYSTEM was updated

Two cross-cutting things changed with the World Cup 1v1 work, both touching the
monetization boundary recorded in [reason_562094](reason_562094.md):

1. **Goal-credit model reversal.** The original rule (PRD Story 26, ADR 0002) said any goal
   that touched the Goalie credits **nobody**, implemented by overwriting the ball's last
   kicker with a Goalie sentinel. In play this felt wrong: a keeper that *fumbles* a save —
   or deflects a hard shot into its own net — robbed the human shooter of a goal they
   clearly earned. We now track **`lastRealKickerAddress`** separately from Goalie touches
   (a Goalie touch never overwrites it) and credit it on *any* goal, including
   Goalie-deflected own-goals. Only a goal with **no** recent human touch (the keeper alone
   knocked it in) credits nobody.

2. **Ephemeral 1v1 Match Pitch loop.** Challenges are raised/accepted from the donut Action
   Wheel and the other-player context menu; accepting spins up an on-demand **Match Pitch**
   room (field template, never persisted), teleports both entrants, runs the pure
   `match.ts` reducer (regulation → golden goal → outcome), and returns both home after a
   short result linger before the empty room tears down.

Both are economic-boundary-adjacent (who gets paid, and where), so they are recorded here
rather than living only in feature code, per the handbook.

## How it serves the larger system

- **Fairness without widening the farm surface.** Crediting the last *human* kicker makes
  saves and deflections feel just, but does **not** loosen the anti-farming guards: the
  keeper still cannot score *for itself* (no human touch → no credit), and the Free Play
  NIM reward stays bounded by the same daily cap, global budget, and Contested ≥2-player
  requirement. The boundary moved from "deflections never pay" to the sharper, more
  defensible "only human-initiated goals pay" — a smaller, clearer rule.
- **Matches stay unpaid by construction.** Private 1v1 Matches still earn **no NIM**; the
  reward path is wired only to the Free Play Field, and Match Pitches are excluded from both
  persistence and the reward path. This preserves the collusion guardrail from
  [reason_562094](reason_562094.md) even as the Match loop ships.
- **Reuses existing seams; stays reversible.** Match Pitches reuse the field bounds / goal
  zones / ball physics and the on-demand room create+teardown path, so no new world-model
  contract is introduced. The Goalie remains a fixed client-side "house keeper" identicon
  (no per-goal owner, no new server field). Everything stays flag-isolated under
  `worldcup/`: `WORLDCUP_ENABLED=0` disables it and deleting the folder plus the
  `worldcup`-tagged hooks removes it entirely.
