# reason_482901 — Pixel paint log and live PNG snapshot

## Why `THE-LARGER-SYSTEM` was updated

Operators asked for a **timelapse-ready history** of the Pixel board and a **shareable live image** of its current state. The authoritative world model only stores the **latest color per tile**; paint order was not retained.

## How this supports the larger system

- **Scale simple → rich:** Reuses existing **`placeExtraFloor` / `baseFloorColors`** authority; adds a **dedicated append-only JSONL** for ordered paints (baseline + deltas) without overloading session analytics logs or bloating `pixel.json` with timestamps.
- **Public artifact without gameplay coupling:** **`/pixels.png`** is a read-only derived view (cached raster), safe to embed and refresh independently of WebSocket clients.
- **Forward constraint:** Baseline-on-first-run makes the timelapse contract honest (no fake history before deploy); future playback applies **`baseline` + `paint` by `ts`**.

- New **Pixel board: ordered paint history + public snapshot** subsection under *Recorded decisions & forward constraints*.
